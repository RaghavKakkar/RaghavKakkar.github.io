MENTAL HEALTH AWARENESS

"What mental health needs is more sunlight, more condor and more unshamed conversation"

Aim of my project is to bring a light on the area of heathcare which is seldom neglected, Mental Heath. With the help of data collected from various sources, I have put up my work on visualizing that data to provide an intuitive insight on Mental health. As it is something that has many factors, different dashboards for each of the issues are prepared.
